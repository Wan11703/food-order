ABOUT US

We opened our doors in April 2024 with a simple mission: to bring you the best drinks possible. Our cafe is all about giving you the freedom to create your perfect beverage. Whether you love coffee, tea, or something more unique, we let you customize every ingredient to match your taste.

At the 12th Avenue Cafe, we believe that everyone deserves a drink made just the way they like it. That’s why we offer a wide range of high-quality ingredients and options for you to choose from. Our friendly staff is always here to help you craft your ideal drink.

Join us at 12th Avenue Cafe and enjoy a personalized beverage experience. We look forward to serving you and helping you create a drink that’s just right for you.
